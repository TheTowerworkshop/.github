# Tower Workshop

**Helping always-on operators modernize mission-critical digital infrastructure.**

Tower Workshop is a fractional CTO practice specializing in platform modernization for mid-market companies where downtime, security failure, or recovery failure creates material business impact. We work alongside MSPs, SOC operators, and executive teams who need disciplined, evidence-backed governance — not just advice.

---

## What We Do

Most modernization efforts fail not because teams lack skill, but because **risk stays implied**. Decisions get made without clear gates, blast-radius controls, or proof points. Eventually, something breaks.

Tower Workshop brings structure to high-stakes platform change:

| Practice Area | What It Addresses |
|---|---|
| **Always-On Modernization (OA3D)** | Fragile, revenue-critical platforms that must change without downtime |
| **Product & Technical Due Diligence (PTDD)** | Investment, acquisition, and founder-readiness decisions |
| **Mission-Critical Risk Assessment** | EOL/renewal/audit forcing functions with explicit cutover governance |
| **Technology Maturity Assessment** | Baseline scoring across six dimensions for deal and strategy decisions |

## Who This Is For

These frameworks are most useful if any of the following are true:

- Incident frequency or severity on a live platform is increasing
- An EOL, renewal, audit, or contract milestone is forcing a decision
- Teams are technically capable but organizationally fragmented
- Leadership is saying: *"If we touch it, it might go down."*
- You are evaluating a technology acquisition or investment and need a structured diligence approach
